Iced Coffee

Whip up a batch of iced coffee at home for you and your crew. This easy iced coffee recipe can be made using your favorite instant coffee. Either way, you get the taste that’s loved by millions…on ice.

Preparation time: 10 mins

Cook time : 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons Classic Roast Instant Coffee
  • 3 cups cold water, divided
  • Ice cubes
  • Sweetener (Optional)
  • Creamer (Optional)

Directions

step 1: Brew coffee DISSOLVE instant coffee in a pitcher of cold water.

step 2: Mix add-ins ADD sweetener and creamer, if using.

step 3: Add Ice SERVE over ice and sip away..

Coffee Cheese cake

this cheesecake recipe is very versatile. Add in tasty flavors like hazelnut, caramel or cinnamon and transform this coffee cheesecake into an ode to your favorite latte.

Preparation time: 30 mins

Baking : 1 hour + chilling

Ingredients

  • Oreo cookies
  • Butter
  • Instant coffee
  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate chips
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Garnish

Directions

Step 1: Prep the pan Preheat oven to 325°F. Place a greased 9-inch springform pan on a double thickness of heavy-duty foil (about 18 inch square) and securely wrap the foil around the pan..

Step 2: Make the crust and prep coffee In a small bowl, combine the cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture onto the bottom of the prepared pan. Set aside.
In another small bowl, dissolve the instant coffee into the hot water. Set aside to cool slightly.

Step 3: Mix the filling In a large mixing bowl, beat together the cream cheese, sugar, flour and vanilla until smooth. Add the eggs and coffee, then beat on low speed until just combined. Pour the coffee cheesecake filling over the crust. Place the springform pan in a large, deep baking pan and add one inch of hot water to the larger pan to make a water bath for the cheesecake.

Step 4: Bake and chill Bake at 325°F for 60 to 70 minutes or until center is just set and the top appears dull.
Promptly remove the springform pan from the water bath and remove the foil. Cool the coffee cheesecake on a wire rack for 10 minutes, then loosen the sides of the pan with a knife. Cool for one hour longer, then cover with plastic and refrigerate overnight.

Step 5: Make the ganache For the ganache, place the chocolate chips in a small bowl. Then, in a small saucepan, bring the heavy cream to a low boil. Pour the cream over the chocolate chips and whisk until smooth. Stir in the vanilla, then cool the ganache slightly until it reaches a spreadable consistency.

Step 6: Decorate and serve Remove the rim from the springform pan. Spread ganache over the coffee cheesecake. Refrigerate for one hour or until the ganache is set. To serve, decorate with whipped cream and espresso beans, if desired, then cut the cheesecake into slices. Enjoy!
